SplitStay Widget Examples

Embed SplitStay widgets on your website to help travelers discover accommodation sharing opportunities.

1. Share Button Widget

A simple call-to-action button that directs users to post their trip on SplitStay. Perfect for travel blogs and booking confirmation pages.

<script src="https://splitstay.travel/splitstay-widget.js" async></script> <!-- Basic share button --> <div class="splitstay-widget" data-type="share-button"></div> <!-- Custom text --> <div class="splitstay-widget" data-type="share-button" data-text="Split Your Hotel Costs"></div>

2. Find Partner Widget

Directs users to browse available trips and find travel partners. Great for travel planning websites and forums.

<div class="splitstay-widget" data-type="find-partner"></div> <!-- Custom text --> <div class="splitstay-widget" data-type="find-partner" data-text="Find Your Travel Buddy"></div>

3. Card Widget

A complete promotional card with title, description, and call-to-action. Perfect for sidebars and dedicated sections.

<div class="splitstay-widget" data-type="card"></div> <!-- Customized card --> <div class="splitstay-widget" data-type="card" data-title="Save on Your Next Trip" data-description="Join thousands of travelers sharing accommodation costs worldwide." data-button-text="Start Saving"></div>

4. Compact Widget

A minimal widget that fits inline with text content. Perfect for blog posts and article content.

Looking to save on accommodation? Try

for your next trip!

<p>Looking to save on accommodation? Try <div class="splitstay-widget" data-type="compact"></div> for your next trip!</p>

Configuration Options

Customize your widgets with these data attributes:

data-type="share-button|find-partner|card|compact" // Widget type data-text="Custom Button Text" // Button text data-title="Custom Title" // Card title data-description="Custom Description" // Card description data-button-text="Custom Button Text" // Card button text data-campaign="your-campaign-name" // UTM campaign tracking
